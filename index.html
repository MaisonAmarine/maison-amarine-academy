Career Hub

Where this world hires.

The private-service world rarely advertises on job boards, it hires through trust, referral, and a handful of specialist agencies. This hub gives you the map: the roles, the pay, and exactly where to look, on both sides of the Atlantic.

Maison Amarine gives you the tools and the map to build your own path, not a placement or guaranteed introductions. The roles and agencies below are real starting points for your own outreach. Listings move fast, always check each agency's live openings.

The roles you're training for
What each one does, and what it typically pays.

Private Concierge / Lifestyle Manager

≈ $70k–$150k+

The right hand of a UHNW individual or family: reservations, travel, sourcing the impossible, anticipating every need. The purest expression of what you learn here.

Maps to: Weeks 1–4 (codes, discretion, anticipation) + Week 5 (toolkit).

Estate / House Manager

≈ $90k–$200k+

Runs one or several luxury residences: staff, vendors, budgets, standards, guest experience. A senior, trusted role, often the top of a household team.

Maps to: Week 5 (operations, staff, budgets) + Week 3 (trust).

Private / Executive PA to UHNW

≈ $80k–$160k+

Manages a principal's calendar, communication, travel and personal life with total discretion. Often blends business and household support.

Maps to: Weeks 3–4 (presence, anticipation) + Week 5.

Guest Relations / Villa & Chalet Host

≈ $55k–$120k+

Front-of-house excellence in private villas, chalets, or ultra-luxury hospitality: welcoming guests, orchestrating their stay, embodying the codes of the house.

Maps to: Weeks 2–4 (codes, presence, service).

Chief of Staff (Private Household)

≈ $150k–$350k+

The strategic head of a UHNW family's entire operation, across properties, staff, and lifestyle. The summit of a private-service career.

Maps to: The full program + years of built trust.

Yacht / Private Aviation Interior

≈ $50k–$130k+

Chief stew, interior crew, or cabin service aboard superyachts and private jets, service at the very highest standard, on the move.

Maps to: Weeks 2–4 (service codes, anticipation).
