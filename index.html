Career Hub

Where this world hires.

The private-service world rarely advertises on job boards, it hires through trust, referral, and a handful of specialist agencies. This hub gives you the map: the roles, the pay, and exactly where to look, on both sides of the Atlantic.

Maison Amarine gives you the tools and the map to build your own path, not a placement or guaranteed introductions. The roles and agencies below are real starting points for your own outreach. Listings move fast, always check each agency's live openings.

Open roles
Real vacancies, live with the agencies that posted them. Click any role to read it in full and apply directly on the agency's own site.

How to use this page. Every role above is a real, published vacancy and every link takes you to the agency's own application page. Maison Amarine is not the employer, does not submit your application, and receives nothing if you are hired. Roles at this level close quickly: if a link has expired, use the live boards below, which are always current.

Every source, in one place
The specialist agencies that place private staff across the US, plus live searches on LinkedIn, Indeed and ZipRecruiter. Bookmark them: they are updated every day and never go stale.

Start where you can actually be taken. These roles form a ladder, not a menu. Coming from luxury hospitality or private aviation, a private concierge or personal assistant role is a natural first step. Coming from elsewhere, guest relations in a villa, a chalet or a yacht is usually the honest first rung, and it climbs quickly. Estate manager and chief of staff are the summit of a career, not the exit of a programme. Choose the rung you can reach, and be impeccable on it.

The roles this craft leads to
What each one does, what it typically pays, and where it sits on the ladder.

Private Concierge / Lifestyle Manager

$70k to $150k+

The right hand of a UHNW individual or family: reservations, travel, sourcing the impossible, anticipating every need. The purest expression of what you learn here.

Your rung: reachable from hospitality, aviation or private assistance. Maps to: Weeks 1 to 5, then Week 7 (candidacy).

Estate / House Manager

median $185k · top quartile $260k+

Runs one or several luxury residences: staff, vendors, budgets, standards, guest experience. A senior, trusted role, often the top of a household team.

Your rung: a senior step, after several years in a household. Maps to: Weeks 3 and 5, and the years that follow.

Private / Executive PA to UHNW

up to $250k at the top of the market

Manages a principal's calendar, communication, travel and personal life with total discretion. Often blends business and household support.

Your rung: a strong first private role. Maps to: Weeks 3 to 5, then Week 7 (candidacy).

Guest Relations / Villa & Chalet Host

$55k to $120k+

Front-of-house excellence in private villas, chalets, or ultra-luxury hospitality: welcoming guests, orchestrating their stay, embodying the codes of the house.

Your rung: the honest first rung if you are new to private service. Maps to: Weeks 2 to 4, then Week 8 (your first ninety days).

Chief of Staff (Private Household)

$150k to $350k+

The strategic head of a UHNW family's entire operation, across properties, staff, and lifestyle. The summit of a private-service career.

Your rung: the summit. Years of built trust, not eight weeks. We show you the path, honestly.

Yacht / Private Aviation Interior

$50k to $130k+

Chief stew, interior crew, or cabin service aboard superyachts and private jets, service at the very highest standard, on the move.

Your rung: an excellent way in, and it travels. Maps to: Weeks 2 to 4 (service codes, anticipation).

The Register

The names that shape this world.

These are the most influential houses in luxury lifestyle management and private concierge: the companies that set the standard, the tone and much of the vocabulary of the world you are entering. You will rarely be employed by them, your roles live inside private households, but you should know them the way an insider does. Being able to speak fluently about this landscape is part of belonging to it.

A quiet note on what this is. The Register is here for your fluency, not your applications. The agencies that actually place private concierges and house managers into families live in your Career Hub. Study these names to understand the industry; go to the Career Hub to find the roles.

The Screening Room

Watch the world you are entering.

The finest way to absorb the codes of private service is to see them. This is a curated list of films, series and documentaries that live inside this world: the great houses, the yachts, the families, and above all the art of serving them with discretion. Watch a few before you finish the program. You will recognise, on screen, everything we teach.
